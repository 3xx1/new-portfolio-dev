As a nature of working for an agency, I can't talk about most of my project details publically online. Below is some sharable projects that I worked on.

Please feel free to reach me out and ask other kinds of work samples.

Ubiquitel

Ubiquitel (a.k.a Finger Drumming Bot Orchestra) is a finger shaped rhythm machine. Sync 100 fingers and play your own finger drumming orchestra!

ProtoPhone

Protophone is a rapid prototyping project to develop a toolkit for user testing in a design research, which was conducted in Africa to prove a phone tree concept.

DropNotes

A tangible user interface for music composition, which utilizes the combination of affordances of familiar objects (droplets, bottles, etc.) as metaphorical procedures to manipulate digital audio.

Trace

Trace is a mobile application that facilitates guided wandering rather than destination-oriented travel.

Arc

Arc is a computer numerical controlled engraving tool for ceramics. It offers a new window onto traditional forms of craft.

Puppetto

Papetto is a lightweight robotic arm that is attachable to anything (such as a phone) and moves according to face detection techniques in order to mirror facial movements.

A/V Pixel

A networked multiple interfaces that brings soundscape into visibility. This tangible acoustic visualization opens up new horizons of audio-visual application.

Shelltone

Mon oreille est un coquillage Qui aime le bruit de la mer (My ears are shells, fondly hearing the sound of the sea), Jean Cocteau composed. Hear soundscape of a digital scenery through shelltone.
